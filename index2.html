About Me

I am 19 years old and I currently study Interaction Design at Ulster University Belfast. I'm from Omagh in County Tyrone but I've been living in Belfast for two years. In my spare time I love to travel and experience the outdoors. Photography is a great interest of mine and needless to say I do a lot of instagramming.

I have always had a keen interest in branding and identity design but through my time as an IxD student I have developed a love for creating illustrations and therefore Adobe Illustrator is one of my favourite pieces of software to use.

I aspire to make my own stamp on the design industry but I'm not quite sure how yet. As I grow as an artist and a designer my style is changing and developing and I'm excited to see where it goes.
